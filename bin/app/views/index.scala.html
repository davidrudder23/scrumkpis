ScrumKPIs is a project to help report on Agile team performance. Normally, a ScrumMaster or software manager would use the tools that come with a ticket tracking system. These tend to lack flexibility because they have a particular Agile structure they use. For instance, you may need to completely close a scrum before you can report on it. This tool allows you to skip a lot of the formality and just get the numbers.
Click on Sprints to get started.
